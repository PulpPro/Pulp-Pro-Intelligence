Feature 02 · All bananas

Colour
Scanner

Point your camera at any banana box. The app detects the colour stage, estimates shelf life, and produces a professional report card you can share in one tap.

Camera viewfinder
Align the banana to the yellow frame. Works with any banana variety — not just Chiquita.
Colour scale (1–6)
From Full Green (1) to Full Brown (6). The active stage is outlined in white on the scale bar.
Stage + shelf life
Colour stage name, shelf life estimate in days, and ripeness status — all calculated instantly.
Share Report Card
Generates a full-quality PNG with the scan photo, colour result and all data — visible at a glance in Teams or WhatsApp.

Get the app

Installed in
30 seconds.

No app store. No download. Open the link, add it to your home screen, enter your access code. Done.

📱
iPhone · Safari
1Open the link in Safari (not Chrome)
2Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
3Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4Open the app and enter your access code
🤖
Android · Chrome
1Open the link in Chrome
2Tap the 3-dot menu in the top right corner
3Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4Open the app and enter your access code

Important: Always open the app from your home screen icon — not from the browser address bar. This keeps the app offline and your access code saved permanently.

Getting access

How to get
your code

Access is by invite only. Each code is personal and one-time use — it can't be passed around.

1
Open the app
Go to the Pulp Pro link. You'll see the access gate — this is normal for first-time users.
2
Tap "Request Access Code"
This opens your email app with a pre-filled message. Before sending, fill in your name, role, and device type (iPhone or Android) — without that info the request can't be processed.
3
Receive your personal code
You'll get a reply with a unique code like JAN-7X#4K — named for you, one-time use only.
4
Enter your code and unlock
Type it into the gate screen. The app unlocks permanently on your device — you'll never need to enter it again.
